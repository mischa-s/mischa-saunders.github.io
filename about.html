I never meant to travel the world. It happened by accident. It was never part of my plan. My plan was to earn a bachelors of political science directly out of high school and go to law school in Toronto where I had grown up. Except I almost didn’t finish high school. The summer after grade eleven I went to work on a farm. That September I didn’t return to school. In the decade since I have visited twenty-three countries and lived in eleven different communities. This has been my education.
